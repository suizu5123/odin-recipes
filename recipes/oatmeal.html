Oatmeal

Description

What I love about oatmeal is that it’s really quick and easy to make, you can customize it with any toppings, and it keeps you full with lots of energy to last your morning! And I love that routine in my morning to have something consistent that I know will make my body feel good.

Ingredients
  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • ½ cup milk of choice
  • ½ cup water
  • Pinch salt

Steps
  1. Place all the ingredients into a medium microwave safe bowl and stir together.
  2. Heat in the microwave on high for 2 minutes. Then add 15-second increments until the oatmeal is puffed and softened. This is only necessary the first time you make it.
  3. Stir before serving.
